9.6 Fork [fork, join] 9 STATE DIAGRAM
9.6 Fork [fork, join]
You can also fork and join using the <<fork>> and <<join>> stereotypes.
@startuml
state fork_state <<fork>>
[*] --> fork_state
fork_state --> State2
fork_state --> State3
state join_state <<join>>
State2 --> join_state
State3 --> join_state
join_state --> State4
State4 --> [*]
@enduml
